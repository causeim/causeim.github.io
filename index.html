SFM

Kinematic and Load-Distribution Analysis of a 4-DOF Parallel Mechanism Built from Paired Coaxial Spherical Five-bar Sets

Jaeho Noh

Manuscript · 2026

I propose the Spatial Five-bar Mechanism (SFM) — a 4-DOF parallel mechanism built from two Coaxial and Symmetric 5R-SPM sets — and a local-index framework (LCI, TAR, and the newly proposed LDI) that quantifies its load sharing, workspace, and end-effector impedance. The radial-sum topology yields closed-form kinematics, ~92% workspace-to-sphere ratio, unbounded coaxial rotation, and broadly uniform load sharing across the four actuators. Two case studies — an industrial arm and a bipedal leg — illustrate the regimes where these properties pay off.
